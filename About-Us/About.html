About Us

With a clear rate of unemployment in Egypt, the difficulty of entering the labor market competition, job requirements of skills and certain abilities have become greater and higher than before, lack of time and abundance of options.
LGH is the most appropriate place to put you on the right path at the least possible time, with the least possible effort and the highest possible efficiency to enhance your capabilities and develop your career through our website and application.


Our Vision

• No one refused from work: our first vision is to help all fresh graduate and young people to be qualified for the job market and never get rejected from job because they are under qualified.
• Get your work as easy as your coffee: our second vision is to help both the non working people and the organizations to find to them a desirable work or the quality of employees they want.


Our Mission

• Enabling the largest possible number of graduates to find their job through agreements and coordination with educational places and companies
• Putting the right person in the right place: by offering organizations a qualified candidates that they really needs and also recommend for job seekers jobs that they can fit with there skills and level of education.
